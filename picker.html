Start generating colour combinations for more information

Initially, both background and text colours are randomly generated.

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to indicate to the network if the colour combination is desirable.

After the network learns at least desirable combos, click the Demonstrate button(UP arrow key) to generate a random background colour and the network will suggest a complementary text colour.

If a weak colour combo is suggested during demonstration, click the text(DOWN arrow key) to give it a new random colour, click Demostrate again and the network will remember the new combo as training.

The likelihood of the network suggesting a strong colour combination will increase with the amount of training.
